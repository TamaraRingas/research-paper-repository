Frequenty Asked Questions (FAQ)

Do you need an account to use the application?

- - The application can be used by anyone. An account is only required if you would like to modify and delete research papers or if you would like to check the proof of peer reviews for a research paper.

How do I create an account?

- To create an account, click on the “Sign-Up” button in the navigation bar. Fill in the form that you get redirected to. Click “Submit” and you will have a user account!

What if I forgot my username or password?

- Click on the “Login” button and select “Forgot Password”. Fill in your email address and the application will automatically email you a reset credentials link.

Can I search for research papers on the webapp?

- Yes! On the Homepage of the application there is a search box titled “Search Research Paper”. Type in the keywords that you would like to search, and press “Submit”, the research paper results will be displayed on the Homepage for you to view. If there are no research papers that match your request, all research papers will be displayed.

Can I filter through research papers on the webapp?

- Yes! On the Homepage of the application there are 4 fields with drop down lists to filter research papers by, and a “Filter” button next to it. The columns allow you to filter for research papers based on the year of publication, the author(s) names, the research group or organisation and the research paper type. Select as many or as few of these options to filter by, and press “Filter”, the research paper results will then be displayed on the Homepage for you to view. If there are no research papers that match your request, all research papers will be displayed.

How do I access the Research Paper Library?

- The Research Paper Library can be accessed by clicking on the “All Research Papers” link in the navigation sidebar.

How do I find a particular research paper?

- There is a search bar at the top of the Research Paper List page. Type in the keywords you would like to search and press the “Search” button and the results will be displayed on the page.

How do I download a research paper from the Research Paper list?

- Click on a research paper’s title on the Research Paper list. This will take you to the Research Paper Detail page of that paper. Click on the “Download” button to download the research paper.

How do I add a new research paper?

- On the Research Paper List page click on the “Add Research Paper” button. This will redirect you to the Add Research Paper Form. Fill in the research paper’s title, author(s) names, year of publication, research group or organisation, the abstract and the actual research paper pdf. Once all information has been completed correctly, click the “Add” button and the research paper will be added.

How do I modify a research paper or its information?

- On the Research Paper Detail page click on the “Modify Research Paper” button. This will redirect you to a form that will allow you to update the details about the requested research paper. Note that you do need the correct permissions to be able to modify a research paper.

What if I upload the incorrect research paper?

- On the Research Paper Detail page, click on the “Delete Research Paper” button to remove unwanted papers. Note again that sufficient user control permissions are required.

How do I share research papers with other users?

- On the Research Paper Detail page, click on the “Share Research Paper” button, you will be redirected to a form where you can select a user to share the research paper with. Click the “Share” button, and the research paper will then be shared with them.

Can I share research papers with external users?

- Yes, at the top right of any research paper there is an icon shaped like an arrow that functions as a share button. When clicked, the research paper pdf document will be available for you to upload. You can attach this document and then click on the “Send Email” option for you to send the research paper to someone’s email.

Can I save a research paper to a personalised library?

- On the Research Paper Detail page, click on the “Share Research Paper” button, you will be redirected to a form with a list of users. Select your username and the research paper will be added to your Research Paper Library, if you have a user account.
